Vdara Hotel & Spa
×

Non-gaming, eco-friendly, all-suite hotel

Access it all
This all-suite, smoke-free CityCenter resort is steps away from ARIA Resort and Casino, and connected to the Bellagio. The ARIA Express tram connects the property to other facilities within the complex.
Full-service spa
The offerings of the Spa & Salon Vdara celebrate the purity and organic freshness of nature. Holistic health, supported by the healing power of nature, is the focus of the spa experience, which can be enjoyed solo or in pairs.
A suite guest experience
Suites at Vdara offer flat-panel televisions and complimentary high-speed wired and wireless Internet access. Kitchens include refrigerators, stovetops, and microwaves.
Bellagio
×

Opulent, Italian-themed Las Vegas resort on lake

Fountains on the Strip
About 1,000 fountains dance to music and light in front of this European-style hotel set along the Las Vegas Strip, next to CityCenter and a tram ride away from Crystals Retail & Entertainment.
Cirque du Soleil
Cirque du Soleil presents the aquatic-based O, with an adjacent show-inspired gallery hosting works by renowned artists.
Premium, newly renovated rooms
Bellagio guestrooms and suites feature semi-canopied beds with leather headboards, pillowtop mattresses, and down comforters; bathrooms offer marble and wood vanities, bathrobes, scales, and phones.
The Venetian Resort Hotel & Casino
×

Opulent Las Vegas all-suite resort and casino with gondolas

On the Strip
This all-suite resort and casino on the Las Vegas Strip is a short drive from Fashion Show Mall, the convention center, Downtown, and McCarran Airport.
Gondolas, shops, spa
The Venetian Resort Hotel & Casino provides an opulent casino, full-service spa, and rooftop pool complex. Guests can attend popular Vegas shows and ride a gondola along Grand Canal Shoppes.
Spacious suites
Guest suites at The Venetian offer sunken living rooms, Italian-marble baths, personal fax/copier/printers. Beds are dressed in Egyptian-cotton linens and white, goose-down duvets. High-speed wired and wireless Internet access is included in the resort fee. Accessible rooms are available on request.
The Palazzo Resort Hotel & Casino
×

Spacious spa, casino, and stylish boutiques on The Strip

Las Vegas Strip
This luxurious resort and casino is on the Las Vegas Strip, connected to sister property The Venetian by walks and waterways. Downtown is 3 miles away and McCarran Airport is within 4 miles.
Full-service spa, casino
The Palazzo provides a spacious, multi-level spa, over 50 international high-end boutiques, a rooftop deck with pools and private cabanas, and a mouthwatering assortment of fine-dining venues.
3 LCD TVs
Guest suites at the Palazzo offer sunken living rooms, work areas with fax/copier/printers, and 3 LCD TVs. Spacious bathrooms have Roman tubs, glass-enclosed showers, and Agraria bath amenities.
Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas
×

Sophistication amid Strip sparkle

Oasis on the Strip
Located on the Las Vegas Strip, the non-gaming Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas provides direct access to the adjacent Mandalay Bay Resort & Casino and to Las Vegas entertainment.
Private pool
Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas is home to a lushly landscaped private pool offering signature amenities such as passed frozen fruit and chilled Evian spritz.
Feeling at home
Inspired by the glamorous Art Deco era, guest rooms are located on floors 35-39 of the adjacent Mandalay Bay tower and floor-to-ceiling windows offer sweeping views of the Las Vegas Strip and surrounding valley.
